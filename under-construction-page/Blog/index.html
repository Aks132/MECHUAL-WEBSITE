Despite being outlawed in 1993, and amended with ‘Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act’ , 2013 , number of manual scavengers and their deaths are unabated!!!!!!! According to 2019 Data by National Commission for Safai Karamcharis , more than 54000 manual scavengers are identified. Safai Karamchari Andolan has pegged death count in thousands since 2000. Approximately, one manual scavenging death every five days, on an average, while cleaning sewers and septic tanks across the country.
